Our team

Anasuya Ghosh

Founder

With several years of industry experience in the departments of sales and hospitality in eminent hotel chains like The Park and Hyatt Regency, Anasuya Ghosh boasts of having managed over 300 events in her 20-year-long career. She also spent considerable time in the department of media sales for the leading radio channel, Radio Mirchi after serving as the Director of Sales for an African media company. In 2012, she founded Morpheus Planners with the aim of reimagining and elevating the event management circuit of Kolkata by curating never-seen-before events for the city of joy. And in 2017, she took yet another step ahead and gave rise to Kanasa Weddings- a vertical of Morpheus that focuses on that one thing she had always been passionate about- weddings!
